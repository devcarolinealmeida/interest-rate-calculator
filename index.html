Interest Rate Calculator

An app that easily calculates how much you need to pay monthly if you need a financing

How to use:

1. Enter the total amount
2. Choose the tax percentage in the predefined buttons or type a custom value
3. Enter the number of installments
**It's done! On the display, you will see the amount per installment and the total amount with interest calculated.
✨ You can customize the total amount, the taxes, and how many installments.
✨ Try changing the number of installments to see in real time the difference in the value of each one.
